🎓 About Me

My name is Aryan Maheshwari, and I’m currently pursuing my M.S. in Applied Data Science at the University of Southern California. Prior to this, I earned a B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, with a specialization in Blockchain, from K.J. Somaiya Institute of Technology.

📌 I'm deeply passionate about building systems that learn, adapt, and make decisions — from intelligent agents to real-time ML pipelines.

Growing up, my curiosity often outweighed caution. I’d take apart devices not to break them — but to understand them. That same curiosity now drives me to explore cutting-edge AI systems, reinforce core math intuitions, and build tools that solve real-world problems.

My journey into tech wasn't linear. It began with solving math puzzles and evolved through hands-on engineering: training Q-learning agents in custom environments, deploying real-time inference pipelines with Celery and FastAPI, and experimenting with GANs for medical imaging. Whether I was debugging PPO agents in MuJoCo or designing retrieval-augmented LLM apps with Milvus and RAPTOR, each project taught me something new — technically and personally.

I’ve been fortunate to work in diverse environments: from leading teams in research internships to contributing to production-ready generative AI systems. My experiences at PayPal, USC Autodrive Lab, and AGIE AI have helped me blend practical problem-solving with academic rigor — optimizing for impact and scalability.

For me, building software is a superpower. It’s about combining logic, empathy, and design to create things that matter. I’m always looking to collaborate on projects at the intersection of AI, decision-making, and real-world utility — especially in domains like healthcare, finance, and robotics.

Let’s build something meaningful together.
